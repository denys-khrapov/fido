About Us . . .

Travel lovers know all the main attractions of popular tourist routes. Seeing them with your own eyes is not a problem. But there are places on earth that for one reason or another are inaccessible to ordinary tourists. And sot you want to look beyond the edge, to know the unknown and see the forbidden - this is the whole human essence. Having tried it ourselves and traveled half the world, we want to share with you our experience and knowledge in traveling to unknown corners of, it would seem, familiar and repeatedly visited countries.

Having visited more than once, both the most exclusive and expensive hotels in the world, and walking with a backpack through the mountain ranges of Peru and spending the night in a hostel or a tent under the open sky, we boldly declare:

"Travel is the road to yourself."

They allow you to get to know yourself again, discover new qualities, and improve your individual skills (these are called soft skills in cool corporations). And we will be happy to help you realize your dream trip or turn an ordinary business trip into an exciting journey.

Be prepared - we will lift the veil of the unknown for you. Ready? Then go ahead…….
